Why choose us

There are many reasons why you should choose us for your removal. Below we have outlined just a few of them;

Local family run business

We are a family run local business, priding ourselves on providing a friendly and personal service with a smile. We aim to make your move as stress free as possible.

Fully Insured

Goods in transit insurance for all items whilst in our care provided by a leading UK insurer, giving you that extra piece of mind. We provide all the equipment needed to carry out your removal in a safe and efficient manor.

Honest prices

We offer competitive and honest prices. We don't waste time whilst 'on the clock', offering excellent value for money for those on a budget.

Every job is important

- No job is too big or too small, but every job is important. We take care of your possessions with the same care respect that we would give our own belongings.

We are based in Penzance, West Cornwall and offer our services the length and breadth of Britain.
