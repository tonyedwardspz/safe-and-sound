House Removals

Whether you are moving across country or just a few streets away, Safe and Sound is the perfect choice. We will get you and your belongings safely to your new home.

We are equipped to handle domestic removals of all sizes. Large family household? Student returning from university? Moving into your first flat? Need a Wardrobe taken to Scotland? Give Safe and Sound a call and make us one of your three quotes..

With our friendly and efficient personal service we aim to make your move as stress free as possible. Our large, long wheel based van is equipped with a modern tail lift. This allows us to load your cherished possessions with the minimum of amount fuss. We offfer a packing service, suppling boxes and packer if required.

We cater to your individual removal needs because we know that no two house moves are the same. We can move all sizes of households or business premises throughout mainland Britain.

Whether it's a single item or all your worldly goods they'll be "Safe & Sound".
